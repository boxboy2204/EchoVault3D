Box Boy and the Blanket Cape

Box Boy wants to be a real superhero, even though he has no powers, a pile of cardboard gadgets that barely work, and a bright blue checkered blanket cape he insists is tactical. Run through a story-mode platformer across city rooftops, clear parkour routes, rescue civilians, and fight through Monarch's crews on the way to the biggest villain in the city.

Controls

WASD / Arrow Keys run

Space jump, then hold to glide

F left punch, G right punch

Enter start or restart

Escape open the city map
