Box Boy and the Blanket Cape

Box Boy is eight years old, dressed in cardboard armor and a bright blue checkered blanket cape, and absolutely certain the house is under attack. Cross three imagination-fueled rooms, rescue his toy crew, and knock back the Vacuum Dragon before bedtime.

Controls

WASD / Arrow Keys move

Space dash with the cape

F unleash Box Burst when imagination is full

Enter start or restart
